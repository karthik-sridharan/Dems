Decision Tree HTML Mini Demos

One page, three quick demos for a lecture: best split, fitting a 2D tree, and overfitting vs depth.

Move the threshold and watch the weighted impurity change. This is good for explaining why the first split matters.

Click inside the square to add points. Train a tree of chosen depth and show the learned axis-aligned regions.
